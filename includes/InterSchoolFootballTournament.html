INTER-SCHOOL FOOTBALL TOURNAMENT



Description: Jesus & Mary Convent School, Greater Noida had organized its 1st Invitational Inter School Football Tournament for under -9 and under- 12 boys from 1st Feb to 5th Feb. The Tournament was held with lot of zeal and enthusiasm. Over ten Schools from Greater Noida participated in the tournament. The final Match for Under- 9 was played between Jesus and Mary Convent School and St. Joseph, in which St. Joseph School won with the lead of 1-0. In the final Match for under- 12 which was played between Somerville School, Greater Noida and St. Joseph. St. Joseph won with the lead of 2-0. Speaking on the occasion, principal Rev Fr. Sebastian congratulated the winners and said that the tournament was Organized to provide an opportunity to the students to develop their love for football. He further said that football is a game that can teach kids how to handle pressure and overcome adversity.
