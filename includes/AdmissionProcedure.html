ADMISSION PROCEDURE

At this stage ‘first come first serve’ shall be followed in case of admission. At present we have started admission in Nursery Class. Education is open for all without any discrimination. Limitations of the child is never looked in to or considered at any cost and in any case for admission.

Registration: The applications forms for admission (Registration) should be obtained from the Jesus and Mary convent school office as well as St. Joseph School office only during office working hours i.e. 8:30 a.m. to 2:30 p.m. No photocopies of the forms will be accepted and no exchange of forms is permitted.

Requirements:

Nursery :
  • Age limit as on 1st April 3+ (Children of age group 2 years 6 months to 3 years and 5 months are eligible to apply)
  • LKG : As on 1st April 4+ (Children of Age group 3 years 6 months to 4 years 5 months are eligible to apply)
  • Recent photograph of the ward with parents should be affixed on the form.
  • Municipal date of birth certificate.
  • An attested copy of the date of birth certificate from the hospital issued at the time of birth.
  • Neither Affidavit nor kundli for/as of date of birth is acceptable. Change of date of birth will not be entertained later.
  • To keep the natural/actual date of birth and not to manipulate it.
Selection: Is based on
  • Introduction /Interactions/meetings with parents on prescribed date
  • Age criteria as mentioned above is the requirement
  • List shall be declared/displayed on the notice Board.(at Jesus and Mary convent School)



Medium of Instruction

Though medium of instruction is English, Hindi is given due importance as a mother tongue and national Language. This is a coeducational Institution with equal number of boys and girls in each class & section.
